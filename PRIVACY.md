# Privacy Policy — Gallery WhatsApp Bot

Last updated: 13 July 2026

This bot is operated on behalf of an art gallery to answer customer
enquiries on WhatsApp.

## What we collect
When you message the gallery on WhatsApp, we store your WhatsApp phone
number, your WhatsApp profile name, the content of your enquiry, and the
artwork you asked about.

## Why
Solely to respond to your enquiry and allow the gallery to follow up
with you.

## Who sees it
Only the gallery. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any
third party for marketing.

## How long
Enquiry records are retained until the gallery has responded and closed
the enquiry.

## Your rights
To have your data deleted, message the gallery on WhatsApp or contact
them directly.
